How to implements Caching
    - The per site cache: Once the cache is setup, the simplest way to use caching is to cache your entire site.
    - The per-view cache: A more granular way to use the cachig framework is by caching the ouptut of individual views.
    - Template fragment caching: This gives you more control what to cache.
